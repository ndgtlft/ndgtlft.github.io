1. Basic Policy

Prompt Stock is an app for saving prompts on your device and copying them when needed. The app does not send your personal information or prompt content to the developer's server.

2. Information Collected

The developer does not collect personally identifiable information, entered prompts, genres, usage activity, or device information through this app.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

Prompts, titles, genres, update dates, copy counts, favorite status, and appearance settings are stored on your device to provide app features. This information is not sent to the developer.

4. External Services, Ads, and Analytics

The app does not use ads, analytics, crash analytics, or tracking SDKs. It does not provide account registration or external server sync.

5. Contact Emails

If you contact us, we will review the information you send by email, such as your message and reply address. We use it only to respond to your inquiry.

6. Apple Services

Downloads from the App Store, purchases, crash reports, and diagnostic information may be handled by Apple under Apple's terms and privacy policy.

7. Changes

We may update this policy when app features, laws, or other circumstances change. Important changes will be announced on this page.
